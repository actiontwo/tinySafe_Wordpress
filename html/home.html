Could cell phone be unsafe? I did want to believe it

Devra Davis, PhD, President and Founder, Environmental Health Trust

Are you are doing everything in your power to protect your baby?

Whether you’re pregnant or have given birth your baby is exposed to radiation every day, this radiation can be harmful at times. To protect your baby it’s often the case where you can just move away from the source of the radiation.

But how can you know when the radiation harmful?

That's where we come in, TinySafe radiation detection device.

TinySafe is a clever device that alerts you of excess radiation in the air.

TinySafe alerts the user in a number of different circumstances including:

  • Your cellphone is exceeding normal levels of radiation emission down to: Age, Type of Reception
  • If a cell phone antenna is too close
  • If a microwave is not safe enough to stand next to

With TinySafe you have one less thing to worry about.
